- **Company blog** - longer-form authority pieces for the Dual Logic site.

## Content pillars

## Goals

- Authority that supports the consulting pipeline (signed clients).

## Format notes
- Voice is governed by `VOICE.md` (hard rules: no em dashes, no "and"-starts, short and punchy).
- LinkedIn: strong first two lines, short lines, no hard sell.
- Blog: scannable subheads, practical, CTA at the end.
- Webinar: teach first, pitch last.

## Hard nos
- No hype-cycle over-promising.
- No AI slop / generic buzzwords.
