Change.org is the world's largest platform for democracy, and we're hiring a VP of Engineering for Change.vote, shaping experiences for voters and elected officials that increase clarity, trust, and participation in democracy. You will shepherd the technical vision along with other pillar leadership and help enable other cross-functional partners to contribute technically to our product. You will report to our CTO. As a key member of our Elections team, you are an AI forward strategic executive leader focused on scaling, maturing and innovating in how the team operates.

At a time when dissatisfaction with democracy globally is at an all-time high, we're investing heavily in using AI to build the most powerful tools in the world to give people greater voice, while bringing people across the political spectrum together to identify shared solutions.

Change.org is building one of the most trusted, accessible, and transparent election information platforms in the United States, Change.vote. In our 2025 pilot, thousands of voters used the app to explore their ballots, with high engagement, strong satisfaction, and a meaningful increase in voter confidence. Our goal for 2026 is bold: scale Change.vote to millions of voters nationwide.

To realize this vision, we're expanding the most talented team in the world at the intersection of technology and social impact - all focused every day on building healthier democracies globally.

Key Outcomes:

 - Clear and strong AI collaboration model between the functions

 - Strong technical leadership as we evolve the ways in which we build our product

 - Deliver significant increases in user engagement and growth through product-driven growth initiatives.

 - Establish a strong, trusted partnership with the VP of Product—jointly owning growth strategy and execution.

 - Influence cross-functional alignment and company-wide direction on growth—establishing best practices and driving coordination across engineering, product, and marketing.

 - Drive Change.vote's data foundation strategy — a master data system reconciling external authorities with AI extraction and human-in-the-loop review to produce a provenanced, product-ready civic data corpus.

The most important core competencies for the role are:

 - Cross functional collaboration and evolution using AI

 - High scale data platform leadership

 - Scaled consumer product experience

 - Building a product that can deal with bursty traffic patterns around elections

Target experience:

 - 10+ years of experience in software engineering, including hands-on coding and architecture.

 - 7+ years in engineering leadership roles, including managing managers.

 - Proven experience leading new product teams or cross-functional squads.

 - Experience scaling products across diverse or international user bases.
