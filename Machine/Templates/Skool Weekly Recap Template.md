## Notes on format
- Tone: warm, community-first, not salesy. Eric's voice — direct, real, no fluff.
- Tag members by name everywhere possible — they get notified and it drives engagement
- Wins section = social proof for free members. Pick 2-3 max, make them punchy
- Threads section = 3-5 max. Link every one.
- YouTube resources = Skool community post URLs work fine if YouTube URLs aren't handy
- Seasonal/milestone callout at top when relevant (hitting round numbers, holidays)
- Close with energy, not a CTA. The content sells itself.
- Red-light safe default: prepare as an internal draft unless Eric explicitly says to post.
